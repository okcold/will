About Me

I grew up in the Cotswolds, surrounded by beautiful architecture and stone buildings. Spending time as a child in and out of listed buildings and churches I naturally grew a fascination with these. As my stepfather got closer to retirement I was honoured to take on the family business.

Years of working alongside my stepfather taught me the art of traditional roofing. Today, I combine those time-honored restoration skills with modern materials for roofs that are built to last without losing their classic character.

I believe every building has its own story. That's why I take the time to understand each property and the people who care for it, tailoring my approach to suit individual needs.
